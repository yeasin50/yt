### Headings

- `# Heading 1`
- `## Heading 2`
- `### Heading 3`

> Up to 6 `#`, used for title, subtitle, sections.

---

### Bold & Italic

- `**Bold**` → **Bold**
- `*Italic*` → _Italic_
- `***Bold Italic***` → **_Bold Italic_**

---

### Lists

- Unordered:

```markdown
- Item 1
- Item 2
```

- Ordered list

```markdown
1. First
2. Second
```

- Task List:

```markdown
- [x] Done
- [ ] To Do
```

---
